Our Story

CodeLab Scheduler was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to make coding education accessible to everyone in Madhya Pradesh.

We noticed that while there were numerous computer labs across educational institutions in the region, students often struggled to get access when they needed it most. The existing scheduling systems were outdated and inefficient.

Our team of local developers and educators came together to create a solution that would streamline the lab booking process, optimize resource allocation, and ultimately help more students develop their programming skills.

Today, we're proud to be partnering with over 50 educational institutions across Madhya Pradesh, helping thousands of students take control of their coding education.
