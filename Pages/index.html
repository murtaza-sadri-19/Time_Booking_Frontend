Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our lab booking system

How far in advance can I book a lab?

You can book labs up to 14 days in advance, depending on lab availability and your account status.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

Cancellations can be made up to 24 hours before your scheduled time without penalty. Late cancellations may affect your booking privileges.

How long can I book a lab for?

Standard bookings are available in 2-hour blocks, with a maximum of 6 hours per day depending on demand.

Can I bring guests to the lab?

Each lab has different policies. Some allow up to 2 guests per booking, while others are restricted to registered users only.
